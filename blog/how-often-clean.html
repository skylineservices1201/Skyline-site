How Often Should You Clean Your Solar Panels?

In Sydney, dust, sea salt, pollen and bird droppings can accumulate quickly. A typical schedule is:

  • Every 6 months for most homes
  • Every 3–4 months if you’re near the coast, main roads, or have heavy bird activity
  • After major storms or construction nearby

Regular cleaning helps restore lost output — homeowners commonly see up to 30% improvement after a thorough clean, depending on soiling levels.
